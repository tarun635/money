Symptons of coronavirus

SYMPTOMS

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to
moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

Three types of symptoms

Less common symptons

  • Acnes and pains
  • Sore throat
  • Lose of taste or smell
  • Headche
  • Conjunctivties

Most common symptons

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Body pains
  • Tiredness

Serious symptons

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Loss of speech or movement

Symptoms of coronavirus.

When to seek emergency medical attention

Look for emergency warning signs* for COVID-19. If someone is showing any of these signs,
seek emergency medical care immediately:

  .Trouble breathing
  .Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  .Inability to wake or stay awake
  .Pale, gray, or blue-colored skin, lips, or nail beds, depending on skin tone

*This list is not all possible symptoms. Please call your medical provider for any other symptoms that are severe or concerning to you.

Call 911 or call ahead to your local emergency facility: Notify the operator that you are seeking care for someone who has or may have COVID-19.
